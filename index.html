1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy describes how Deviatkov Dmitrii Evgenievich (the "Developer", "we") processes data in the "StepMotivator" app (the "Application").

Important: This Application is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All step counting is performed locally on your device using the built-in accelerometer sensor.

2. Data We Collect

2.1. Sensor Data (Local Only)

  • Accelerometer Data: Used exclusively to count your steps. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

2.2. Notifications

  • Hourly Reminders: The Application sends local notifications every hour to motivate you to move. These notifications are generated by your device's operating system and do not involve any data transmission over the internet.

3. Third-Party SDKs and Advertising

We do not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The Application contains no ads and does not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data (step count, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the Application, all associated data will be permanently deleted from your device.

5. Your Rights

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the Application or clearing app data in your device settings.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can revoke permissions (e.g., notification access) at any time via your device settings.
